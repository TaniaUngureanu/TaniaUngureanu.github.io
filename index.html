    Get to know me

    Born and raised in Brasov, I had big dreams growing up. I was committed to become a singer, until people told me I couldn't sing! So, I did the only other thing I could: I went to school. I followed a carrier in economics; not out of passion, but because it was an adequate path at the time. It was not that bad at all, and I was rather good at it, but I soon lost the excitement of having a job and being an adult. So, I started looking for ways to fill a certain void in my life. I took up some german lessons, I passed the Chartered Accountant Exam, I became a real estate appraiser somewhere on the way, until I finaly found it:

    I am a developer wanna be!
